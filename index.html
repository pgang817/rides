Best places to visit in Hyderabad
Charminar
<
The Charminar, built in 1591 by Sultan Mohammed Quli Qutb Shah, stands at the intersection of the old city’s main streets. Its four grand arches symbolize the entry points of the city, while its minarets rise to 56 meters. The surrounding area, Laad Bazaar, is famous for its vibrant market selling traditional jewelry, textiles, and other goods. The monument is also a significant cultural landmark, representing Hyderabad’s architectural splendor and historical significance.
>
Best places to visit in Hyderabad
Golconda
<
Golconda Fort, originally built in the 13th century by the Kakatiya dynasty and later expanded by the Qutb Shahi rulers, is renowned for its formidable fortifications and unique acoustic system. The fort’s sound system was so advanced that a clap at the entrance could be heard at the top of the fort. Inside, visitors can explore the remnants of the royal palaces, gardens, and the imposing outer walls. The fort also offers a panoramic view of the city and its surroundings.
>
Best places to visit in Hyderabad
Ramoji Film City
<
Ramoji Film City, spread over 2,000 acres, is one of the largest film production facilities in the world. Established in 1991 by Ramoji Rao, it offers a range of attractions including themed gardens, live shows, and film sets. Visitors can explore film sets, participate in studio tours, and experience the magic of filmmaking. It also has entertainment zones, adventure activities, and a variety of dining options.
>
Best places to visit in Hyderabad
Salar Jung Museum
<
Established in 1951, the Salar Jung Museum is one of the most prominent art museums in India. It houses over 43,000 artifacts, including sculptures, paintings, manuscripts, and textiles. The museum’s collection spans various periods and cultures, featuring works from Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. Highlights include the famous Veiled Rebecca statue, a remarkable marble sculpture by Giovanni Maria Benzoni.
>
Best places to visit in Hyderabad
Birla Mandir
<
Constructed between 1976 and 1978, the Birla Mandir is a modern Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara. Built from white marble, the temple is known for its intricate carvings and serene atmosphere. Located on a hill, it offers panoramic views of the city. The temple complex includes a beautiful garden and an array of deities, making it a peaceful retreat and a center for spiritual activities.
>
Best places to visit in Hyderabad
Shilparamam
<
Shilparamam is an arts and crafts village located in the heart of Hyderabad’s cultural district. It showcases traditional crafts and art forms from various regions of India. Visitors can explore artisan stalls, attend cultural performances, and participate in workshops. The village also features a range of traditional Indian handicrafts, textiles, and jewelry, providing an authentic cultural experience.
